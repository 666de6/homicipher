Homicipher Dictionary(English): Decoding the Mysterious Language

Last Updated: November 20, 2024

Hey fellow players! After countless hours of gameplay and lots of note-taking, I've put together this comprehensive Homicipher Dictionary guide. Trust me, figuring out all these translations wasn't easy - it took tons of playthroughs, careful attention to context clues, and cross-checking with other players in the community to make sure everything's accurate.

In this guide, I've tried my best to provide the most accurate translations for every word and phrase I've encountered. While you might find slightly different meanings that work better for you (and that's totally fine!), these are the translations that made the most sense to me and seemed to fit best with the game's context.

⚠️ Heads up!
As a player who's already gone through multiple playthroughs, I strongly recommend experiencing the game's language mysteries on your own first. Half the fun is in figuring things out yourself! This guide contains major language spoilers that might take away from your first playthrough experience.
