Welcome to the Homicipher Wiki, your comprehensive guide to the unique horror adventure game that combines elements of language deciphering with survival horror. Players must navigate through a mysterious world while learning and mastering an alien language system.

Gameplay

The core gameplay mechanics that revolve around deciphering mysterious messages while avoiding dangerous entities. Players must manage resources, solve puzzles, and make crucial decisions that affect their survival.

Core Mechanics

  • Language System - Master the unique communication system to interact with other characters
  • Survival Horror - Navigate through dangerous environments while managing limited resources
  • Puzzle Solving - Decipher complex codes and solve environmental puzzles
  • Character Interactions - Build relationships with various entities that affect game progression

Game Progression

  • Multiple Endings - Your choices and relationships influence the story's outcome
  • Skill Development - Improve your language deciphering abilities throughout the game
  • Environmental Storytelling - Uncover the world's mysteries through exploration

Development

Homicipher was developed by 八名木（yatsunagi）and published by Gamera Games. The game began development with the vision of creating a unique horror experience that combines language puzzles with survival elements.

Development History

  • Initial Concept - The game's unique concept of combining horror with language deciphering was established early in development
  • Development Period - The game underwent thorough development to perfect its core mechanics and atmosphere
  • Trial - Scheduled for release on June 1, 2023 on Steam platform
  • Release - Scheduled for release on November 1, 2024 on Steam platform

Technical Features

  • Custom language system for in-game communication
  • Atmospheric horror elements and sound design
  • Puzzle mechanics integrated with narrative progression

Characters

The game features several mysterious entities, each with their own unique behaviors and communication patterns. Learn more about each character in our detailed character guide.

  • Mr. Hood - A mysterious Grim Reaper-like figure who serves as your initial guide, teaching you the basics of the world's strange language
  • Mr. Crawling - Your ultimate ride-or-die companion who will do anything to protect you, despite his unsettling appearance
  • Mr. Silvair - A scholarly figure with a sensei-like presence who serves as one of your most effective language teachers
  • Mr. Machete - A powerful presence who values strength above all, showing interest only in those who prove their worth
  • Mr. Gap - A chaotic but reliable ally who appears in critical moments, providing crucial information about your past
  • Mr. Chopped - An endearing character who maintains a cheerful personality despite adversity, known for being particular about his hair
  • Mr. Scarletella - An enigmatic pursuer whose true identity and motives remain shrouded in mystery
